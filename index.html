The Panel

Multi-AI Debate
Direct Client Client-Side App

One question. A panel of AI. A single synthesized answer.

Avoid the bias and blindspots of a single model. The Panel lets Anthropic, OpenAI, and free tools like Gemini and Groq debate your question in multiple rounds—defending, refuting, and refining arguments until a neutral judge synthesizes the final honest verdict.

Uncover Gaps & Errors

Putting AI models face-to-face forces them to challenge each other's claims. Inaccuracies and weak reasoning don't survive a cross-examination round, leading to more robust outputs.

Completely Free Alternatives

No paid API keys? Generate free keys for Google Gemini or Groq to host debates using different models or personas under the same provider for zero cost.

Secure & Direct Browser Calls

The app acts as a local console. Your API keys are kept securely in your browser's session memory, going directly to providers. No servers in the middle.

How the Panel Operates

01

Independent Answers

Every active model receives your question in isolation. Models start from their default biases with no access to peer responses, providing their initial raw perspective.

02

Cross-Examination Rounds

Each model receives a compiled transcript of arguments from the other models during the previous round. They are instructed to critique their peers, defend their original points, or adapt their consensus views based on convincing evidence.

03

The Synthesis & Verdict

A designated judge model reads the cross-examination history. The judge synthesizes the debate into a single direct answer, outlining the winning points, identifying unresolved conflicts, and noting if the models legitimately disagreed.

Your Privacy & API Key Safety

  • Direct Request: Your browser contacts the provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Groq, OpenRouter) directly. There are no intermediate backends.
  • Storage Options: By default, keys are held purely in the temporary session script object. Toggling the "Keep keys active" option stores keys in sessionStorage, which is destroyed automatically the moment you close the tab.
  • Zero Tracking: The application does not collect analytic data, queries, or results. All histories are stored completely inside your local browser storage.
